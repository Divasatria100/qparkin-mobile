# QPARKIN Booking Feature - User Guide

## Overview
The Booking feature allows you to reserve parking slots at your favorite malls before you arrive, ensuring you always have a spot waiting for you.

---

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- QPARKIN mobile app installed
- Active account (logged in)
- At least one vehicle registered
- Internet connection

---

## How to Book a Parking Slot

### Step 1: Select a Mall

1. Open the QPARKIN app
2. Navigate to the **Map** tab
3. Browse available malls on the map or switch to **Daftar Mall** tab for a list view
4. Tap on a mall to see details:
   - Mall name and address
   - Distance from your location
   - Available parking slots
   - Operating hours
5. Tap **"Booking Sekarang"** button

### Step 2: Select Your Vehicle

1. On the Booking page, you'll see a **"Pilih Kendaraan"** section
2. Tap the dropdown to see your registered vehicles
3. Select the vehicle you'll be using
4. Each vehicle shows:
   - License plate number
   - Vehicle type (Mobil, Motor, etc.)
   - Brand and model

**Note:** If you don't have any vehicles registered:
- Tap **"Tambah Kendaraan"** button
- Fill in your vehicle details
- Return to booking page

### Step 3: Choose Booking Time

1. In the **"Waktu Mulai"** section:
   - Tap to open date/time picker
   - Select your arrival date and time
   - Default is 15 minutes from now
   - You can book up to 7 days in advance

2. In the **"Durasi"** section:
   - Choose from preset options: 1h, 2h, 3h, 4h
   - Or tap **"Custom"** for specific duration
   - Minimum duration: 30 minutes
   - Maximum duration: 12 hours

3. The **"Waktu Selesai"** (end time) is calculated automatically

### Step 4: Check Slot Availability

The **"Ketersediaan Slot"** indicator shows real-time availability:

- **Green (>10 slots):** Plenty of slots available
- **Yellow (3-10 slots):** Limited slots available
- **Red (<3 slots):** Very few slots available

**Features:**
- Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds
- Tap the refresh icon for manual update
- Shows last check time

**Tip:** If slots are unavailable, try:
- Adjusting your arrival time
- Reducing booking duration
- Selecting a different mall

### Step 5: Review Cost Estimate

The **"Estimasi Biaya"** card shows:

1. **First Hour Rate:** Base parking fee
2. **Additional Hours:** Rate for each extra hour
3. **Total Estimate:** Your total parking cost

1. Tap the **"Konfirmasi Booking"** button at the bottom
2. Wait for confirmation (usually takes 2-3 seconds)
3. A success dialog will appear showing:
   - Booking confirmation number
   - QR code for entry
   - Booking details

**What happens next:**
- Your booking is saved
- Parking slot is reserved
- QR code is generated
- You receive a notification

## After Booking

### View Your Booking

1. Tap **"Lihat Aktivitas"** in the confirmation dialog
2. Or navigate to **Activity** tab
3. Your active booking is displayed with:
   - Mall location
   - Vehicle information
   - Booking time range
   - QR code for entry
   - Countdown timer

### Using Your QR Code

**At Mall Entrance:**
1. Drive to the parking entrance
2. Open QPARKIN app
3. Go to **Activity** tab
4. Show your QR code to the scanner
5. Gate opens automatically
6. Park in your assigned slot

**Important:**
- Keep your phone charged
- QR code works offline
- Don't share your QR code
- Arrive within your booking time

### Modifying Your Booking

**Current Limitations:**
- Bookings cannot be modified after confirmation
- To change details, you must cancel and rebook

**Cancellation:**
- Go to Activity tab
- Tap on your active booking
- Select "Cancel Booking"
- Confirm cancellation
- Refund policy applies

## Tips for Best Experience

### Timing
- **Book in advance** during peak hours (weekends, holidays)
- **Arrive on time** - your slot is reserved for your booking window
- **Allow buffer time** - add 30 minutes to your expected duration

### Cost Savings
- **Book exact duration** - you pay for the full booking even if you leave early
- **Extend if needed** - additional charges apply if you overstay
- **Check rates** - different malls have different pricing

### Slot Availability
- **Check real-time availability** before booking
- **Be flexible** with timing if slots are limited
- **Consider alternative malls** if your first choice is full

### Vehicle Management
- **Keep vehicle list updated** - remove sold vehicles
- **Add all vehicles** - for easy selection
- **Verify details** - ensure license plate is correct

## Troubleshooting

### "Slot tidak tersedia"

**Problem:** No parking slots available for your selected time

**Solutions:**
1. Try a different time (earlier or later)
2. Reduce booking duration
3. Select a different mall
4. Check again in a few minutes (slots may become available)

### "Anda sudah memiliki booking aktif"

**Problem:** You already have an active booking

**Solutions:**
1. Complete or cancel your existing booking first
2. Check Activity tab for active bookings
3. Contact support if you believe this is an error

### "Koneksi internet bermasalah"

**Problem:** Network connection issue

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Switch between WiFi and mobile data
3. Move to an area with better signal
4. Try again in a few moments

### "Sesi Anda telah berakhir"

**Problem:** Your login session expired

**Solutions:**
1. Log out and log back in
2. Restart the app
3. Check your account status

### Booking Not Showing in Activity

**Problem:** Booking confirmed but not visible

**Solutions:**
1. Pull down to refresh Activity page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Restart the app
4. Contact support with booking ID

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance can I book?
**A:** Up to 7 days in advance.

### Q: What's the minimum booking duration?
**A:** 30 minutes.

### Q: What's the maximum booking duration?
**A:** 12 hours per booking.

### Q: Can I extend my booking?
**A:** Not currently. You'll need to make a new booking or pay additional charges when exiting.

### Q: What if I arrive late?
**A:** Your slot is reserved for your booking window. If you arrive after your start time, you can still use it until the end time.

### Q: What if I leave early?
**A:** You'll still be charged for the full booking duration.

### Q: What if I stay longer?
**A:** Additional charges apply based on the mall's hourly rate.

### Q: Can I cancel my booking?
**A:** Yes, through the Activity tab. Refund policy applies.

### Q: Do I need internet to show my QR code?
**A:** No, the QR code works offline once your booking is confirmed.

### Q: Can I book for someone else?
**A:** Yes, as long as you have their vehicle registered in your account.

### Q: What if the QR code doesn't scan?
**A:** Contact the parking attendant or use the manual entry option with your booking ID.

### Q: Are there any booking fees?
**A:** No, you only pay the parking rate. No additional booking fees.

### Q: Can I book multiple slots?
**A:** No, only one active booking per user at a time.
